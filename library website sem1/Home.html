ABOUT FICTION

Fiction books open the doors to boundless imagination and endless, Whether you are a seasoned reader or just beginning to explore the world of literature, fiction books offer something for everyone. At HarperCollins India, we curate a diverse collection of fiction books that cater to all tastes and preferences. Fiction books are narratives created from the imagination of the author, encompassing various genres such as romance, mystery, fantasy, science fiction, and more.

ABOUT HISTORY BOOKS

History books play a crucial role by serving as repositories of collective memory, helping to understand the present by revealing patterns in human development, and providing insights into our identities. They educate by showing past mistakes to avoid repetition, inspire through historical events, and foster critical thinking and empathy by exploring complex issues from multiple perspectives.

ABOUT TECHNOLOGY

Technology books in libraries cover everything from the basics of library tech management (planning, systems) to specific skills for librarians (programming, AI, data) and patron-focused resources on digital literacy, AI, machine learning, programming (Python, Java), and data science, reflecting libraries shift to digital hubs offering ebooks, databases, and internet training. These resources empower both staff and users in navigating our increasingly digital world, from understanding AI to using programming for data tasks.

FOR KIDS

The role of sci-fi and fantasy is to provide escape, explore "what if" scenarios, and examine complex real-world issues through imaginative settings. These genres allow readers to experience different perspectives, travel to other worlds, and consider possibilities for the future, from the utopian to the dystopian, fostering imagination and critical thinking about society, technology, and human nature.

ABOUT HUMOUR

Humor play a multifaceted role in personal well-being, social interaction, and cognitive development. They serve not only as a source of entertainment and stress relief but also as a powerful tool for exploring complex social issues and fostering empathy.

BIOGRAPHY AND MEMOIR

Biography and memoir books provide readers with valuable insights into different lives, historical contexts, and human experiences, fostering empathy, education, and motivation. They offer lessons on success and failure, expand worldviews by exposing readers to diverse people and perspectives, and allow readers to vicariously experience the struggles and triumphs of others. Memoirs provide a more intimate, emotional perspective on specific life events, while biographies aim to present a comprehensive, factual account of an entire life within a broader historical context.
